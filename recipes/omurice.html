Tony's Omurice Recipe

Why this recipe?

  • It's easy!
  • It's yummy! egg soft and fluffy and taste buds likey :)
  • It's for the lazy :D

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs (more if you luv eggs)
  • tomato paste - Can be substituted with ketchup
  • Sriracha - do you even need to question this?
  • cooked rice - better if leftover
  • chicken thigh - thighs over breasts >:)
  • garlic - begone devil and hello smelly breath
  • butter - adds creamy taste to rice and ingredients
  • onions - yummy when sautéd
  • soy sauce - juicy flavours
  • green onions - can be substituted if poor

Procedures

  1. Chop up the onions into rings and throw them into the pan to glaze them in medium heat. Add soy sauce, a tiny amount of salt ( or even no salt since soy sauce is already salty), and some pepper as the onions cook.
  2. Add finely diced garlic before the onions cook up. Then add in your green onions and some butter.
  3. Dice your chickens into small or medium sized cubes and add them to the pan.
  4. Wait until chickens are no longer pink before adding your rice.
  5. Add some more butter as well as tomato paste and Sriaracha as needed. Add more soy sauce to enhance flavour until satisfactory.
  6. Put serving on plate once rice is fried well.
  7. Now the most important part: THE OMLETTE. Sadly I have a 6% winrate on this so you're gonna have to search up a tutorial on this.
  8. Done!
